Site Plan - Sandy Utah Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

Our purpose is to help the local business owner be successful and build community and unity in our city. We want to be a resource for those just starting out, for those businesses that have a few years under their belt, and for those that have been around for a while that maybe need a boost in patrons. We want all to be successful and for our community to succeed!

Target Audience

Our target audience are local business owners in Sandy Utah. We want all business owners to feel like they have a resource with us. We welcome businesses of all shapes and sizes. Come be an active participant and build a strong business with us.

Senarios

  • How can I grow my business?
  • What is the best way to avertise?
  • How do I get found on google?
  • Are there affortable business classes I can take to help me manage my small business?
