Plan

Purpose

This isn't just your normal Dick and Jane weather site. For the outdoor enthusiast there will be suggestions on where to hike for the day, avalanche safety news and suggestions on where NOT explore. We will list the ski resorts in the area sorted by most snow received in the last 24 hours. You will also find local news stories to keep you updated and informed. The pupose of the site is to be more than just a weather site, but to be a one stop shop for his or her day.
